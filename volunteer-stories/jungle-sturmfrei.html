The work

What I actually did

Content Planning Graphic Design Video Shoots Paid Ads Office Space Design Event Organizing

Joined Jungle Sturmfrei's social media team as a graphic designer and content planner. Shot and edited short-form videos, designed all social creatives, and built a posting system that kept the feed consistent without the grind.

Beyond digital — I helped design their Delhi office space. Mood boards, wall art direction, signage, and visual identity for the physical space. The kind of work that blurs the line between brand and place.

Growth

4,000 to 7,100 followers

Grew the Instagram from 4K to 7.1K through consistent original content — no gimmicks. A mix of reels, event recaps, and community moments that actually reflected what the place felt like to be in.

"Best People Best Times" — not just a caption. That's the entire content strategy.

Paid Marketing

42% to 76% occupancy

Ran paid campaigns for three months — lead gen and occupancy. Targeted the right traveler profile, kept CPL tight, and backed it up with creative that didn't look like an ad. Occupancy climbed from 42% to 76% over the campaign window.
